# Identity LoRA Sample Prompts - Evaluation Set
# Trigger word: TOK Woman
# These 5 prompts are designed to evaluate identity preservation quality
# across different conditions: lighting, environment, style, and pose.

# 1. CLEAN PORTRAIT - Baseline identity test
# Tests: Basic facial features, skin tone, eye color, hair
TOK Woman, professional headshot photo, plain white background, soft studio lighting, sharp focus, looking directly at camera, neutral expression, 8k uhd

# 2. DRAMATIC LIGHTING - Identity under challenging light
# Tests: Identity preservation with strong shadows and highlights
TOK Woman, portrait with dramatic side lighting, dark background, chiaroscuro lighting, half face in shadow, moody atmosphere, professional photography

# 3. OUTDOOR ENVIRONMENT - Identity in natural context
# Tests: Identity in a completely different setting than training
TOK Woman, candid street photography, golden hour sunlight, urban city background, natural pose, bokeh background, authentic moment, lifestyle photography

# 4. CINEMATIC STYLE - Artistic style transfer
# Tests: Identity preservation while applying strong stylistic changes
TOK Woman, cinematic film still, anamorphic lens flare, teal and orange color grading, shallow depth of field, movie scene, 35mm film grain, directed lighting

# 5. EXPRESSIVE POSE - Identity with different expression
# Tests: Identity across facial expressions and body language
TOK Woman, laughing genuinely, candid happy moment, warm natural lighting, lifestyle portrait, joyful expression, eyes crinkled from smiling, authentic emotion
